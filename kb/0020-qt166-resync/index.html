Resync a QT166 Remote

2014-11-24

When the batteries die completely in a QT166 remote, it loses its sync with its TV. These are instructions to re-sync it1:

  1. Charge up remote for 5 hours
  2. Switch on remote
  3. Switch on TV using remote
  4. Switch off remote for 2 seconds and switch on again
  5. Press and hold the RF button on the keyboard side of remote for 4 seconds
  6. Plug in the RF dongle to TV USB port
  7. Place remote next to TV and wait for a few minutes for pairing to complete

  1. I forget where I found these, it was many years ago. ↩︎
